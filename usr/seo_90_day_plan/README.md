# 90-Day B2B SEO & Digital Marketing Execution Plan

Welcome to your comprehensive 3-month execution plan. This structured toolkit is designed to help a B2B company improve website traffic, generate qualified leads, and increase search engine rankings.

## How to Use This Plan

This plan is structured into 7 modular CSV files. These can be opened individually in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or Apple Numbers, or you can import all of them into a single `.xlsx` workbook as separate sheets.

### 1. Master Dashboard (`1_Master_Dashboard.csv`)
**Purpose:** Provides a high-level overview of the 3-month goals and top-level KPIs.
**Action:** Update the "Current Actual" and "Status" fields at the end of each week to keep stakeholders informed of overall progress toward the traffic, lead, and conversion targets.

### 2. Daily Activity Plan (`2_Daily_Activity_Plan.csv`)
**Purpose:** The core day-to-day operational schedule from Day 1 to Day 90.
**Action:** The project manager or team lead should review this sheet daily. Assign tasks, adjust priorities, and change the "Status" to Completed as the team executes the 90-day sequence.

### 3. Blog Content Plan (`3_Blog_Content_Plan.csv`)
**Purpose:** An editorial calendar featuring targeted B2B SEO blog topics, primary/secondary keywords, and search intent.
**Action:** Content writers use this to guide their weekly drafts. Ensure the "Internal Linking Plan" column is updated once URLs are live.

### 4. Keyword Tracking (`4_Keyword_Tracking.csv`)
**Purpose:** A dedicated sheet for monitoring search engine ranking positions (SERPs).
**Action:** Update the "Current Ranking" and "Previous Ranking" weekly using data from Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google Search Console to measure SEO growth.

### 5. Lead Gen & Outreach Tracking (`5_Lead_Gen_Outreach.csv`)
**Purpose:** A CRM-lite tracker for cold email and backlink outreach efforts.
**Action:** Sales or Lead Gen reps must update this sheet daily with the companies contacted, follow-up dates, and the current lead status to ensure opportunities don't fall through the cracks.

### 6. Technical SEO Checklist (`6_Technical_SEO_Checklist.csv`)
**Purpose:** A recurring checklist to ensure the website remains fast, healthy, and technically sound.
**Action:** The web developer and SEO specialist should complete these checks on their designated weekly or monthly frequencies.

### 7. Monthly Performance (`7_Monthly_Performance.csv`)
**Purpose:** A granular month-over-month reporting sheet.
**Action:** At the end of Month 1, Month 2, and Month 3, extract data from Google Analytics and your CRM to fill this out. Use the "Action Plan" column to correct course for the upcoming month.

## Getting Started

1. Open `1_Master_Dashboard.csv` and adjust the target numbers to fit your specific baseline metrics.
2. Review `2_Daily_Activity_Plan.csv` and assign the "Responsible Person" roles to your actual team members.
3. Begin execution on Day 1!
